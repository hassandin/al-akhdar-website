About Al-Akhdar

Founded in 1958, Al-Akhdar Sports Club has been a cornerstone of Libyan football for over six decades. Based in Al-Bayda, our club has consistently demonstrated excellence both on and off the field, nurturing talent and bringing pride to our community.
